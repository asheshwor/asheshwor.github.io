Bio

Hello, I’m Asheshwor and I do many things. Currently, I am with the Department of Geography, Environment and Population, University of Adelaide working on my PhD on the topic of human-environment interactions and climate-resilience in the Nepali plains. I have finished my primary field-work in Nepal and I am expected to finish by early 2017.

Apart from the university, I also volunteer at the Adelaide City Library Innovation Lab tutoring patons about 3D printing, 3D modelling and robotics.

Educational background

  • PhD (ongoing), Department of Geography, Environment and Population, The University of Adelaide, Australia
  • Master in Urban & Regional Planning [MURP] (2010), University of Hawai’i at Mānoa, Honolulu, Hawaii, USA. Specialization in environmental planning and urban design
  • Leadership Certificate (2010), East-West Center Education Program / University of Hawai’i at Mānoa, Honolulu, Hawaii, USA
  • Bachelor in Architecture [B. Arch.] (2004), Tribhuvan University, Institute of Engineering, Pulchowk Campus, Pulchowk, Lalitpur District, Bagmati Zone, Nepal. Elective courses in energy efficient design
